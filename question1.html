PARUL UNIVERSITY



Parul University is a private university in Vadodara, Gujarat, with a new campus in Goa, offering over 250 courses across various disciplines like engineering, pharmacy, and management. It is known for its extensive campus, strong industry connections, and placement opportunities, with the main campus in Vadodara receiving an A++ NAAC grade.

Key Details

  • Location:
    Main campus in Vadodara, Gujarat, with a new campus established in Goa in 2025.
  • Accreditation:
    Achieved an A++ grade from NAAC and a gold rating from QS I-Gauge.
  • Academics:
    Offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, diploma, and certificate programs.
